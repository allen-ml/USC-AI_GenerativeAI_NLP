Generative AI and Applied Machine Learning for Natural Language Processing (NLP)

ITP-459 • Fall 2024 • University of Southern California

Welcome to ITP 459 - Generative AI and Applied Machine Learning for Natural Language Processing (NLP) at the University of Southern California. This course is designed to immerse students in the rapidly evolving fields of Generative AI and NLP, providing hands-on experience with cutting-edge machine learning techniques. Throughout the semester, you'll explore how generative models are transforming industries, from text generation to conversational agents. By the end of the course, you'll have developed a solid understanding of the theoretical foundations and practical applications of AI in NLP, equipping you with the skills to tackle real-world challenges in this exciting domain.
